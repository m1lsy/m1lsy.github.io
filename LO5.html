PERSONAL LEADERSHIP

LEARNING NEW SKILLS INDEPENDENTLY

What I Did:

I showed my personal leadership and my commitment to learning through out the semester by going the extra mile and teaching myself new skills. These skills include learning new programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop and Blender.

ILLUSTRATOR
Before beginning this semester, I had no experience with illustrator and blender and some very beginner level skills in photoshop. Design is something that has always been appealing to me and something that I am passionate about and so I was very excited to begin the process of exploring these new programs.

I learnt the basics of Illustrator by watching YouTube tutorials to help me navigate my way around the program. I also attended a workshop taught by Jan where he gave a beginners crash course to the program. With this basic knowledge, I was able to complete my first logo design for Geldrops MuziekCorps in the first rebranding project.

PHOTOSHOP
In regards to Photoshop, I have had some previous experience in High School playing around with it for fun projects but no serious experience. As with Illustrator, I watched a few youtube tutorials and I also asked my father for help as he has some experience with it.

BLENDER
I was extremely excited to begin my journey with 3D modelling this semester in Blender as it is something that has always fascinated me. My excitement got the best of me and I started playing around with it before my first lecture and tried to follow the famous donut tutorial by Blender Guru. Unfortunately, I gave up due to the program constantly crashing as soon as I tried to edit my shape.

RONIN GIMBAL
I also learnt how to use a Ronin Gimbal through experimenting with it for my second group project. Together as a team, we got it from the ISSD and set it up and played around with it. It was quite complex and very new to me, but it was very exciting to play around with a new device like this.

What I learned:

Through these various processes, I learnt how to navigate new programs and learnt the basic skills of each one. I also learnt how to use new equipment which also taught me to use the ISSD more as there are so many amazing devices and pieces of equipment for us to use.
